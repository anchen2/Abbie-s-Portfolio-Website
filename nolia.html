Nolia: Eco-Friendly Travel Sized Containers

Branding & Packaging

Project Overview

Nolia is a sustainable packaging company that designs reusable containers designed for traveling. We offer product such as 3oz reusable containers, clear toiletry bags, water bottles and more. All products are made from 100% recycled materials and are designed to last years. We have a range of 3oz containers the perfect size to take in a carry one. We have all types of containers for all types of products, shampoo, toner, perfume, lotions, and cream.

The Problem

Due to the rising cost of living, many young adults face financial concerns and must carefully plan their travel for school or visiting family. At the same time, they are increasingly mindful of their environmental impact. These factors impact the sustainable choices they make when traveling.

How Can We Statement

How can we encourage travelers to contribute less to the environment while traveling without incurring additional expenses?

Research Insights

  • Cost of Travel: A major concern for young adults when traveling is the cost. And eco-tourism is seen as expensive.
  • Why People Travel: Young people travel for lesiure and to visit family.
  • Sustainability Awareness: Young people are aware of only a few ways to travel sustainably.
  • What is Eco-Tourism: Eco-Tourism allows people to have more awareness of the effects of travel on the environment.
